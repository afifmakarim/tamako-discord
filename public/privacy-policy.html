Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data Tamako Bot collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

  • User/Server IDs: Essential for processing commands.
  • Message Content: Temporarily processed when interacting with the AI via mentions.
  • Third-Party Data: Processed when explicitly requested (Steam, Dota).

2. Data Retention and Sharing

Tamako Bot does not log or store your message history or personal content in any permanent database. Data is processed transiently in memory. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

3. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can stop using the bot or remove it from your server at any time, ceasing all data collection.
  • If you do not mention the bot or run its commands, it ignores all chatter in the server.
